Qiskit Fall Fest

We are thrilled to invite you to one of the most exciting global events in quantum computing, brought to you by IBM Quantum: the Qiskit Fall Fest! This year, the event will be hosted by the Military Technical Academy "Ferdinand I". Join us in Bucharest as we unite universities, enterprises, students, and quantum enthusiasts from across the country. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced Q-scientist, there's a place for you in our vibrant community. Let's explore the quantum frontier together at the Qiskit Fall Fest 2024!

Qiskit Fall Fest is a collection of quantum computing events that invites students, researchers and industry professionals around the world to participate in a wide array of quantum-themed activities, ranging from quantum challenges, hackathons, and coding competitions to workshops, social events, and more. With each Qiskit Fall Fest, we partner with a select group of university students and other volunteer hosts to help them plan and run the global roster of Fall Fest events. This year's event theme, World of Quantum, celebrates the international scope of the event series and the rapid growth of the global quantum community.

FAQ

Who is this event for?

This event is for anyone interested in quantum computing, from complete beginners to advanced experts, including students, researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts. A background in linear algebra and (Python) programming is recommended.

What is the cost of attending the event?

Participation in the event is free, however, accommodation during the hackathon will not be provided.

Where is the event located?

The event will be held at the Military Technical Academy “Ferdinand I” in Bucharest, Romania. Check our venue page for more details.

What are the event dates and times?

The event will take place from October 14 to November 24, 2024. Details can be found on our schedule page.

In what format will the event be held?

The event will consist of two components, the online part where courses will be held and tasks will be assigned in the form of coding challenges and the in-person part where participants will propose innovative solutions for various problems using quantum technologies in a hackathon.

Is the attendance in the online courses mandatory?

No, but it is strongly encouraged as you will use that knowledge for solving the hackathon challenges.

How can I contact the organizers for other questions?

Once you are accepted as a participant, you will receive an invitation to the official Discord server.

What should I bring to the in-person hackathon?

Bring a valid ID for registration, your laptop, and be prepared to learn while having fun.

Are meals provided during the event?

Yes, meals and refreshments will be provided. Please indicate any dietary restrictions during registration.

Is there parking available at the venue?

No, the inside parking is available only for personnel at the Military Technical Academy.

How can I become a sponsor or partner for the event?

We welcome sponsorship and partnership inquiries. Please contact us for more information on sponsorship opportunities.

Will there be prizes for the winners?

Yes, the prizes will be announced with the kickoff of the Hackathon. There will also be surprise prizes and awards, so stay tuned!
